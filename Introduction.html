Introduction

Welcome to my blog! I'm a postdoctoral fellow at Emory University researching how hemodynamics influence the initiation and progression of atherosclerosis. My research journey has resulted in me finding myself at the intersection of biomechanics, computer vision and data science.

Here I will be detailing my data science experiences and offering tips and solutions on working with Tensorflow. My first post will deal with importing sequences of images into Tensorflow using the Dataset API. Stay tuned!
